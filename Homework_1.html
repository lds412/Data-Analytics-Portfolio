When we fit a regression model to the code above where y is the response variable and x1, x2 are the regressors, the coefficient of x1 is 1.4396 (somewhat close to its true value of 2) and the coefficient of x2 is 1.0097 (a little less close to its true value of 0.3). The coefficient of x2 has a p-value of 0.3754 which is not considered statistically significant. This is because the correlation between x1 and x2 make it hard for R to accurately sort out and attribute the correct influence of x1 and x2 on y.
